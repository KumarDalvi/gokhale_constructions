ABOUT US

You lead the way when you stay a step ahead.

In 2004, when the real estate segment in Pune was expanding to newer locations, a unique need was taking shape- upgrade of the residential spaces at the most prominent locations in Pune. Identifying this need and understanding the desires of the people before anyone else, kicked off the journey of Gokhale Constructions.

Since then, Gokhale Constructions has been the trendsetter in upgrading spaces at Pune’s most loved locations, namely, Aundh, Prabhat Road, Ideal Colony, Mayur Colony, Erandwane, Sahakar Nagar, Subhash Nagar, Karve Nagar and many more. With 175+ projects dotting these locations, Gokhale Constructions is the preferred choice of Punekars.
