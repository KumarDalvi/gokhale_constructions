What is Self Redevelopment?

Self redevelopment is a process in which the residents take on the responsibility of redeveloping the society, rather than the builder. While there are many projects undergoing redevelopment currently in Pune, self-redevelopment is set to be the next chapter that will reimagine redevelopment. With multiple advantages, from space planning to profits and timelines, self development is set to be a gamechanger.

RISE by Gokhale (Redevelopment Initiative for Self Empowered) will provide a comprehensive solution to self development in Maharashtra as per the new Development Control Regulations (DCR).

The Process of Self Redevelopment

Split across 3 stages and 16 well-defined steps, self redevelopment takes care of every aspect carefully.

Stage 1

  1. SELF REDEVELOPMENT CONSENT:

    2. Consent of 100% society members to take up self redevelopment.

  2. INITIATE THE PROCESS:

    3. The society to approach RISE with all the required details and documentation.

  3. FEASIBILITY STUDY:

    4. Structuring the project cost and revenue in a manner to maximise benefits for the society.

  4. APPOINTMENT OF RISE:

    5. Getting written consent for appointment of RISE and signing the necessary agreements.

Stage 2

  1. DESIGN DEVELOPMENT:

    2. Preparation of unit plan, layout and elevation as per the requirement of members.

  2. LAND & REVENUE WORKS:

    3. Obtaining the title search report and preparation of various undertakings required for approvals.

  3. BUILDING PERMISSION:

    4. Obtaining necessary approvals from respective authorities for architectural plans.

  4. PROJECT FINANCE:

    5. Arrangement of loan from financial institutions. based on the project report.

  5. RERA REGISTRATION:

    6. Obtaining the RERA registration and uploading all the relevant documents including the project's Commsencement Certifcate.

  6. VACATING THE PREMISES:

    7. Moving out the existing society members on receipt of Commencement Certificate and handing over of the premises to RISE.

Stage 3

  1. SALES & MARKETING:

    2. Preparing and finalising a comprehensive sales strategy for additional units.

  2. CONSTRUCTION & MONITORING CASH FLOW:

    3. Ensuring quality and workmanship, and monitoring timely completion of the project.

  3. POSSESSION & ENROLLMENT OF NEW MEMBERS:

    4. Handing over the premises to old & new members on obtaining Occupancy Certificate and handing over corpus to the society.
